Well come to the most finest Centre in the world for the 5-a-side football. There is 10 pitches and hosting over 10000 and more games, you can't go wrong with this centre. It is not just a play ground it is also a social hub for the people that want to meet and have fun. There is just so much to do. With 100 of staff working around the clock to give you the best experience. You can book a place or higher a pitch for a fun game with friends or family.

With fully licensed function suites for hire across all our venues, choose Goals for your next special occasion.
From birthdays to anniversaries, engagement parties, weddings and christenings, or even a staff night out or football presentation evening, our versatile events space can be adapted to meet all your needs.
Our dedicated and experienced events team are on hand to help you with catering, decorating and music options, leaving you to enjoy the event stress free.
