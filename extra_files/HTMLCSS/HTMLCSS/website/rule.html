Rules & Regulations

follow this and you will be good to go...

Players

1: A11 player should be full kitted, This includes proper shoes and shin pads.
2: A team should have 5players in a team or they are not allowed to continue and will automatically loose.
3: A team can have up to 3 substitutes per game.
4: Player should respect the calls of a referee.
5: If a players act in an offensive or a disrespectful manner they have a chance of being kicked out of the league.
6: Players should inform the referee before making a substitutes.

Match

1: Match last for 20minutes, 10minutes each half.
2: If its a draw in the knock-out stage you will go to a penalty kick-out where 3 players form each teams take in turn to decide a winner.
3: A match can be cancelled if one of the teams is unable to turn up, automatically giving the team that turned up a 3 points.
4: There will be a 5minutes max in-between the half and if the players are ready before the time they can start straight away.
5: Wining a match 3points, loose a match 0point and draw 1points.
6: If you have 100points or more in a season you are able to choose to go up a league or stay in the same league.

rules that referee should go through before every match

1: No over head ball is allowed00.
2: Must inform the referee before a substitutes is made.
3: No slide tackles are allowed.
4: Goalkeeper are not allowed out of the D-box.
5: All free kicks are indirect.
6: Respect the calls.
